The BIVV Method

Brain, Identity, Voice, Video — in 72 hours

DAY 1

Brain Extraction

  • • 2-6 hours of deep interview and ideation
  • • Extract frameworks, philosophies, stories, and IP
  • • You talk, we structure. Record and transcribe everything.
  • • Output: A structured "Brain File" with content pillars, signature stories, and your unique language
DAY 2

Voice & Avatar

  • • Read selected scripts and speak naturally
  • • Model emotional range (serious, excited, authoritative)
  • • Capture reference angles, lighting, wardrobe
  • • Multiple voice profiles if needed (podcast, ad, training)
DAY 3

Assembly & Calibration

  • • Run your Brain File through the system
  • • Test multiple scripts (short-form, long-form, authority)
  • • Adjust until your twin feels like you
  • • Live reaction session for final calibration
