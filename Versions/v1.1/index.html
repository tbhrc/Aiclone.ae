The BIVV Method

Brain, Identity, Voice, Video — in 72 hours

Day 1 • Brain

Deep Brain Extraction

2-6 hours of guided interview to extract frameworks, philosophies, stories and IP. Output: Your structured "Brain File".

Day 1 • Identity

Persona Mapping

Define how you want to be seen. Set tonal rules. Align wardrobe, backdrop and visual cues.

Day 2 • Voice

Voice Cloning

Read scripts, model emotional range. Create multiple voice profiles: serious, excited, intimate.

Days 2-3 • Video

Avatar Creation

Capture angles, lighting, expressions. Train avatar for multiple scenes. Calibrate until it feels like you.
