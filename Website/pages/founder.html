From executive coaching to AI innovation

For over two decades, David Potgieter has been extracting the stories, frameworks, and unique perspectives of high-performing individuals — CEOs, founders, and elite professionals.

His career spans executive recruitment, leadership coaching, and consulting for some of the region's most influential business figures. The through-line? Helping people articulate who they are and what they stand for.

AIClone.ae is the natural evolution of that work — taking the art of personal brand extraction and applying it to cutting-edge AI technology.

Why I created AIClone.ae

The idea came from watching the same pattern repeat over years of consulting: incredibly talented leaders with valuable frameworks and insights, stuck on the content treadmill.

They knew they needed to be visible. They knew their IP was valuable. But they couldn't possibly record enough videos, write enough posts, or be in enough places at once.

When I saw what was becoming possible with AI voice cloning and avatar technology, I realized there was a way to solve this problem — but only if done with the same care and precision I bring to executive coaching.

AIClone.ae isn't a tech product. It's a consulting-first experience that happens to use AI. The 72-hour process feels more like working with a biographer than a software vendor.
