Day-by-Day Breakdown

1

Day One

Brain & Identity Extraction

Brain Extraction Session

2-6 hours of deep interview and ideation that extract your frameworks, philosophies, IP and signature stories.

  • Record and transcribe everything
  • Structure content pillars and models
  • Produce your "Brain File"

Identity & Persona Mapping

Define who you are on screen. King, magician, strategist, mentor. How bold vs discreet, formal vs conversational.

  • Clarify market perception goals
  • Set tonal rules for your twin
  • Align wardrobe and visual cues
2

Day Two

Voice & Image Capture

Voice Cloning Session

Record scripts in quiet locations. Capture different emotional modes: teaching, conversational, persuasive, reflective.

  • Serious, excited, reflective modes
  • Refine pacing and emphasis
  • Test on sample scripts

Avatar Image Capture

Capture high-quality visual data. Plan outfits, environments and lighting for realistic, respectful avatar training.

  • Range of expressions and angles
  • Serious and relaxed looks
  • Select best frames for training
3

Day Three

Calibration & Handover

Twin Assembly & Testing

Train video avatar, test output with short scripts. Configure multiple scenes: office, studio, stage, neutral.

  • Check lipsync and body language
  • Generate test clips together
  • Refine until it feels like you

Handover & Training

Walk your team through how to operate the twin. Deliver brain file, starter videos, and 30-day content plan.

  • Team training session
  • Simple usage guide
  • Content calendar outline
