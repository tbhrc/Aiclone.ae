# AIClone.ae – Product Requirements Document (PRD) v1.0

## 1. Product Overview

AIClone.ae is a premium, Dubai-based white-glove service that creates Digital AI Twins for busy founders, executives, and personal brands. The product combines deep knowledge extraction ("brain dumps") with avatar and voice cloning to enable clients to scale their personal presence across platforms — without recording content daily.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

**Target Audience:**  
- CEOs and Founders  
- Thought Leaders & Coaches  
- Influencers with inconsistent content pipelines  
- Real estate & high-trust B2C entrepreneurs

**Core Problems Solved:**  
- “I don’t have time to record content.”  
- “If I don’t post 3 videos a day, I lose reach or income.”  
- “I want to productize my knowledge, but don’t know how.”  
- “My brand relies on *me*, but I can’t scale myself.”

---

## 3. Key Features

### 3.1 Brain Extraction (BIVV)
- Human-led interviews and strategy mapping
- Collects client documents, videos, frameworks, CVs
- Output: structured Notion-based "Brain File"

### 3.2 Voice Cloning
- ElevenLabs-powered voice model  
- Multiple tone variations for context-based output

### 3.3 Avatar Creation
- HeyGen or similar engine  
- Multiple virtual sets: office, green screen, villa, etc.  
- 100+ outfit/background configurations

### 3.4 Content Generation
- Team feeds clone scripts based on brand voice  
- Automated daily/weekly content production  
- Output: vertical video (Reels), horizontal (YouTube), blog voice notes, etc.

---

## 4. Service Workflow (Client Journey)

| Stage | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **1. Waitlist/Signup** | Apply via aiclone.ae, book 15-min intake |
| **2. Brain Session Booking** | 72-hour slot confirmed (1 client per block) |
| **3. BIVV Session** | Filmed at client’s villa, studio, or jet |
| **4. Voice & Avatar Creation** | Tools used: ElevenLabs + HeyGen |
| **5. Twin Deployment** | Clone tested on short scripts |
| **6. Content Calendar Setup** | 1-month plan auto-generated |
| **7. Ongoing Output** | Team manages weekly updates, reviews, edits |
