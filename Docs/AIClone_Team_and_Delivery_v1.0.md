## 3. Internal Delivery Roles

### 3.1 Project Manager (can be shared role)
- Coordinates the 72-hour schedule
- Manages pre-session documents and travel/recording logistics
- Acts as client concierge

### 3.2 Clone Architect (David or Senior Consultant)
- Leads brain extraction session using context engineering
- Interviews client, extracts models, beliefs, story
- Maps frameworks into the “Brain file”

### 3.3 Avatar Tech Specialist (Dan or AI Ops)
- Operates avatar setup: voice, visuals, rendering
- Integrates voice model from ElevenLabs
- Coordinates HeyGen or comparable engine setup
- Ensures script-to-video testing is complete

### 3.4 Editor & Asset Producer (Support)
- Clips, polishes and renders all videos for client delivery
- Applies templates, subtitles, aspect ratios
- Delivers export folder in usable formats

## 6. Operational Backbone

- **Delivery Toolkit:** Notion + Zoho Writer + Claude + ElevenLabs + HeyGen  
- **Scheduling:** Zoho Bookings (for session times)  
- **CRM & Pipeline:** Zoho CRM (shared with iMPLEMENTAi.ae)  
- **Asset Storage:** Zoho WorkDrive or Google Drive  
- **Internal SOP Library:** Notion master space with versioning  

---

## 7. Scalability Principles

- **One Client Per 72h Rule:** Maintains luxury + exclusivity  
- **Clone Architects must be trained consultants** (not just AI users)  
- **No automation replaces interview process** — keep the premium edge  
- **Delivery = white-glove.** Every touchpoint must feel bespoke
